Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Client-Side Processing

Verticon Tools operates with a privacy-first approach. All file processing (PDF merging, splitting, image conversion, etc.) occurs entirely within your web browser using technologies like WebAssembly and JavaScript.

We do not upload your files to any server. Your documents never leave your device.

2. Data Collection

Since we do not host a backend for file processing, we do not collect, store, or share your documents.

We may collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., page views) to improve the user experience, but this data is never linked to your uploaded content.

3. Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage (IndexedDB) to temporarily hold files while you are working on them. This data is stored on your device and can be cleared by you at any time.
